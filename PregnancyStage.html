Understanding Pregnancy Stages

A comprehensive guide through your 40-week journey of pregnancy, from conception to birth.

First Trimester

Weeks 1-12

Fetal Development

  • Fertilized egg implants in the uterus
  • Heart starts beating (weeks 5-6)
  • Basic organs begin to form
  • Arms, legs, and facial features develop

Mother's Changes

  • Morning sickness
  • Fatigue and mood swings
  • Tender breasts
  • Increased urination
  • Food cravings/aversions

Health Tips

  • Take prenatal vitamins
  • Avoid smoking and alcohol
  • Limit caffeine intake
  • Maintain balanced diet
  • Stay hydrated

Medical Checkups

  • First ultrasound scan
  • Blood tests for iron levels
  • Immunity screening

Second Trimester

Weeks 13-26

Fetal Development

  • Baby begins to kick and move
  • Organs continue maturing
  • Baby starts hearing sounds
  • Fingerprints and hair develop

Mother's Changes

  • Baby bump becomes visible
  • Increased energy levels
  • Better appetite
  • Skin changes

Health Tips

  • Light exercise (walking, yoga)
  • Sleep on left side
  • Increase calcium intake

Medical Checkups

  • Anomaly scan (20-week)
  • Gestational diabetes screening

Third Trimester

Weeks 27-40

Fetal Development

  • Rapid weight gain
  • Lungs complete development
  • Moves into birth position

Mother's Changes

  • Back pain and leg cramps
  • Braxton Hicks contractions
  • Shortness of breath
  • Difficulty sleeping

Health Tips

  • Practice breathing exercises
  • Prepare hospital bag
  • Monitor baby's movements

Medical Checkups

  • Weekly checkups after week 36
  • Monitor baby's position
